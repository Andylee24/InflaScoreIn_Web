Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

InflaScore is a wellness tracking app for daily lifestyle behaviors. This policy explains what information the app may collect, how it is used, and the choices available to you.

Information you provide

You may enter lifestyle records such as meals, sleep duration and quality, exercise, stress level, supplement use, hydration, display name, avatar, and optional health profile details such as age, biological sex, height, and weight.

How information is used

Your information is used to calculate your anti-inflammatory lifestyle score, show daily summaries, display trends, and support app settings such as reminders.

Local storage

The current app is designed as a local-first experience. Data is stored on your device using Apple platform storage. We do not operate a custom cloud server for syncing personal health logs in this prototype stage.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, the app may schedule local notifications. You can manage notification permissions in iOS Settings.

Health and medical disclaimer

InflaScore is for wellness and lifestyle tracking only. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment, and it does not measure inflammation directly.

Contact

Questions can be sent to support@inflascore.app.
