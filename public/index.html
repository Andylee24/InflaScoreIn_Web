Six Daily Dimensions

A fast check-in that still feels complete.

No calorie database. No overbuilt dashboards. Just the lifestyle signals that matter for a practical anti-inflammatory routine.

01 🥗 Diet

Log anti-inflammatory, neutral, and pro-inflammatory foods with simple portion awareness.

02 😴 Sleep

Track duration and subjective quality so recovery doesn't disappear into memory.

03 🏃 Movement

Capture low-impact activity, strength, yoga, or training without overcomplicating it.

04 🧘 Stress

Note your nervous-system load with four lightweight states from calm to overwhelmed.

05 💊 Supplements

Record optional basics like fish oil, vitamin D, magnesium, or probiotics.

06 💧 Hydration

Keep your daily water target visible and easy to update throughout the day.
